Hi, my name is William Graham, and if you're reading this it means that I was able to complete all the things I wanted to do with this project before the due date. That also means that there are four pages worth of funny jokes that'll make any student at PTC go 'Yeah, I felt that.'. This page was meant more as a meta insight on the distractions and possible threats to a proper education that are posed to online students.

This website has four pages, those pages being Janky Internet, Blackboard, ADHD, and Video Games; video games can also be called vidya, if you are so inclined. Each of these things pose a serious threat to the average PTC student's education, and many of them are unavoidable or hard to resist. In each page will be a description of the threat and ways to avoid the threat without hurting yourself or your grade.
