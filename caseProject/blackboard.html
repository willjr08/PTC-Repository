The best way to handle Blackboard and it's jank is to realize that Blackboard is a habitual liar. If your attendance is wrong, it's because attendance is wrong on Blackboard 50% of the time. Informing your teachers of Blackboard Jank through email is a great way to be spared the hammer of a lowered grade.
